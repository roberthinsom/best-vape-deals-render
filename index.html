Understanding the Canadian Vape Market

The Growth of Vaping in Canada

The vaping industry in Canada has seen remarkable growth due to increasing awareness of vaping as a potential smoking cessation tool and the variety of flavors and devices available. With the legalization and regulation of vaping products by Health Canada, consumers can access safer and more reliable products.

Key Regulations to Know

  • Age Restrictions: You must be at least 19 years old to purchase vaping products in most provinces.
  • Product Standards: All vape products must meet safety standards set by Health Canada.
  • Flavor Bans: Some provinces have specific restrictions on flavored vape juices, which may impact availability and deals.
